🐾 Welcome to PawMatch Shelter

Every animal deserves a loving home — and every person deserves a loyal friend.

Our Mission

PawMatch is a non-profit animal shelter that helps abandoned and rescued pets find their forever families. We believe in compassion, care, and second chances. Here, every wagging tail tells a story of hope.

Our Story

PawMatch Shelter was founded in 2017 by a small group of animal lovers who couldn’t walk past abandoned pets on the streets of Almaty. What started as a small backyard rescue for a few stray dogs quickly grew into a full-fledged shelter, home to hundreds of animals. Over the years, more than 1200 dogs and cats have found their forever homes thanks to our volunteers and kind donors.

Today, PawMatch operates as a non-profit organization dedicated to providing medical care, food, and a safe temporary home for abandoned and injured animals. Our shelter is located in a peaceful green area near the mountains — a place filled with warmth, barking, and purring.

How We Care

Every animal that comes to PawMatch receives medical examination, vaccination, and, if necessary, rehabilitation before being put up for adoption. Our volunteers spend hours walking the dogs, cleaning their enclosures, and helping them regain trust in people.

We believe that love heals. Many of our animals come from difficult circumstances — rescued from the streets, abusive owners, or even disaster zones. With time, care, and patience, they become happy, social, and ready to meet their new families.

Our Community

PawMatch is run by a team of 25 volunteers, veterinarians, and coordinators who work tirelessly every day. In partnership with local clinics and schools, we organize events and educational programs about kindness, adoption, and responsible pet ownership.

Our Impact

- 🐾 Over 1200 pets adopted into loving homes.
- 🏡 Currently 75 animals under our daily care.
- 💊 More than 200 animals receive medical help every year.
- 💖 Countless hearts healed — both human and animal.
